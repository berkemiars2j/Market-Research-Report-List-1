<p><p>EVA infusion bags are categorized into single chamber and multi-chamber types, each serving distinct medical needs. Single chamber bags are designed for one type of solution, ensuring simplicity and ease of use, typically used for straightforward infusions. In contrast, multi-chamber bags contain multiple compartments that allow for the combination of different solutions or medications, ensuring stability and extended shelf life. This versatility is essential for complex treatments requiring multiple nutrients or medications administered simultaneously.</p></p>
