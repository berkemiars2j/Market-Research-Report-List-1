<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nonwoven Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meltblown Type</li><li>Spunbonded Type</li><li>Spunlace Type</li><li>Needle Punch Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonwoven sheet market consists of various types, each serving different applications. Meltblown type fabrics are characterized by their fine fiber structure, making them ideal for filtration and insulation. Spunbonded types are robust and commonly used in hygiene products and geotextiles due to their strength. Spunlace types are created through hydroentangling, offering softness and comfort for wipes and medical textiles. Needle punch types use mechanical agitation to bond fibers, suitable for carpets and automotive interiors. Other categories include composites and specialty nonwovens for diverse applications.</p></p>
