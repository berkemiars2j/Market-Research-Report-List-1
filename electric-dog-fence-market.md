<p><p>The electric dog fence market is primarily divided into two types: above-ground and in-ground systems. Above-ground fences are typically easier to install, using visible wires or flags to define boundaries, which can be adjusted as needed. In contrast, in-ground fences involve buried wires that create an invisible boundary, providing a more discreet solution. Both types use collars to deliver a warning or correction when a dog approaches the set perimeter, ensuring safety without the need for traditional physical barriers.</p></p>
