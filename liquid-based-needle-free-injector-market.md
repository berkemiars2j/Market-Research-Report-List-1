<p><p>The liquid-based needle-free injector market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Jet injectors** use high-pressure to deliver medication through the skin in a fine spray.</p><p>2. **Spring injectors** rely on a spring mechanism to propel the liquid medication at high speed into the tissue.</p><p>3. **Laser injectors** utilize focused energy to create a micro-opening in the skin, allowing the drug to penetrate.</p><p>4. **Vibration injectors** employ rapid vibrations to enhance drug delivery by disrupting the skin barrier. </p><p>These technologies provide painless alternatives to traditional needles, enhancing patient comfort.</p></p>
