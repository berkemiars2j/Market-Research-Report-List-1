<p><ul><li>Laser-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li><li>PMP-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line 3D solder paste inspection equipment is essential for ensuring the quality of solder application in electronics manufacturing. Two primary types are laser-based and PMP-based systems. Laser-based 3D inspection utilizes laser technology to measure the height and volume of solder paste, providing precise data for quality control. PMP-based systems employ a projection method, capturing images to analyze paste placement and accuracy. Both technologies help detect defects early, enhancing production efficiency and product reliability.</p></p>
