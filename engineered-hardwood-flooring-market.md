<p><strong>The Engineered Hardwood Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tongue-and-groove (TandG) Technology</li><li>Click-locking Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered hardwood flooring market features two primary installation technologies: tongue-and-groove (T&G) and click-locking. T&G technology involves planks that fit together using a tongue and groove system, allowing for secure connections and a traditional appearance. This method often requires glue or nails for stability. In contrast, click-locking technology utilizes a simple interlocking mechanism for easier, glue-less installation, making it popular for DIY projects. Both types offer durability and aesthetic appeal, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
