Membrane-to-membrane systems utilize a barrier membrane to prevent leakage and ensure safe transfer during drug preparation and administration. Needleless systems eliminate the need for needles, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and enhancing safety. Both types are designed to maintain a closed environment, protecting healthcare workers and patients from potential exposure to harmful substances while ensuring efficient drug delivery.</p></p>
