<p><p>Transportation Management Software (TMS) can be categorized into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise TMS is installed and maintained locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for management. In contrast, cloud-based TMS is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs while allowing for easier updates and maintenance. Both types aim to optimize transportation operations and improve efficiency.</p></p>
