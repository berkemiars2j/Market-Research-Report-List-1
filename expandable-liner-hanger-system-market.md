<p><p>The Expandable Liner Hanger System market is categorized into two main types: Mechanical Systems and Hydraulic Systems. Mechanical Systems utilize a series of mechanical engagement methods to secure a liner within the wellbore, ensuring stability during drilling and production. In contrast, Hydraulic Systems rely on fluid pressure to expand and anchor the liner, providing a more versatile and often simpler installation process. Both systems are designed to enhance well integrity and optimize production efficiency while accommodating various geological conditions.</p></p>
