<p><strong>The Cassava Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dried Cassava Pulp</li><li>Wet Cassava Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cassava pulp market is categorized mainly into dried and wet cassava pulp. Dried cassava pulp is produced by removing moisture, resulting in a lightweight, long-shelf-life product suitable for animal feed and food applications. It is valued for its nutritional content and ease of storage. Wet cassava pulp, on the other hand, retains moisture and is primarily used in the food industry for fermentation, as well as in livestock feed, offering higher freshness and nutritional quality but with a shorter shelf life.</p></p>
