<p><p>The large size cooling fan market includes various types primarily categorized as axial fans, centrifugal fans, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis, making them efficient for cooling large spaces. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, direct airflow at right angles to the intake, providing higher pressure for ventilation in industrial applications. Other types encompass specialty fans designed for specific needs, such as high-temperature environments or specialized processes, ensuring versatile cooling solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
