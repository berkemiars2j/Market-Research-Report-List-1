<p><p>The conveyors market encompasses various types tailored to specific material handling needs. Roller conveyors utilize cylindrical rollers to transport items efficiently, ideal for heavy loads. Belt conveyors use a continuous belt for smooth product movement, suitable for diverse applications. Overhead conveyors maximize space by suspending items above the floor, often used in manufacturing. Pallet conveyors are designed for transporting palletized goods, enhancing logistics. Other conveyor types include spiral, modular, and chain conveyors, catering to specialized needs across industries.</p></p>
