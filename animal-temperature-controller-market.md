<p><p>The Animal Temperature Controller market primarily consists of three types: AC, DC, and AC/DC systems. AC controllers utilize alternating current to maintain stable environmental conditions, often suited for larger facilities. DC controllers are powered by direct current, offering efficiency in smaller or mobile setups. AC/DC systems combine both technologies, providing flexibility and adaptability for varied applications. Each type caters to specific needs in livestock management, ensuring optimal temperature and comfort for animals, which in turn impacts their health and productivity.</p></p>
