<p><ul><li>Coronary Stents</li><li>Peripheral Stents</li><li>Stent-related Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stents and related implants market is categorized into coronary stents and peripheral stents. Coronary stents are used to treat blockages in the coronary arteries, improving blood flow to the heart. Peripheral stents target arteries outside the heart, such as those in the legs or arms, to enhance circulation. Stent-related implants include drug-eluting stents that release medication to prevent restenosis and bioresorbable stents that dissolve over time, offering temporary support while promoting natural healing. These innovations are vital for effective vascular treatments.</p></p>
