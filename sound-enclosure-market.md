<p><p>The sound enclosure market includes three main types: fully enclosed, active, and partially closed systems. Fully enclosed enclosures are designed to completely surround sound sources, effectively isolating noise. Active enclosures utilize technology to actively reduce sound levels through noise cancellation techniques. Partially closed systems, on the other hand, offer some degree of sound isolation while allowing limited airflow or access, making them suitable for specific applications where complete enclosure is not feasible. Each type addresses unique noise control needs and environments.</p></p>
