<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Time-resolved Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-thyroglobulin Antibody Test Kit market encompasses two primary types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay and Time-resolved Immunoassay. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes light emission to detect antibodies, providing high sensitivity and rapid results. In contrast, Time-resolved Immunoassay employs a time-dependent signal decay to enhance detection accuracy, offering reliable quantitative analysis. Both methods are crucial for diagnosing autoimmune thyroid disorders, facilitating early detection and management of conditions such as Hashimoto's thyroiditis and thyroid cancer.</p></p>
