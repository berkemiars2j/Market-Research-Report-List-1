<p><p>The plastic dome lids market encompasses several types primarily categorized by the resin material used in manufacturing. Polypropylene (PP) lids are flexible, resistant to heat, and often used for takeout packaging. Polystyrene (PS) lids are rigid, lightweight, and cost-effective, making them popular for cold beverages. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) lids are clear, strong, and recyclable, often used for fresh food applications. Additional materials may include copolymers or bio-based plastics, catering to specialized packaging needs and sustainability preferences.</p></p>
