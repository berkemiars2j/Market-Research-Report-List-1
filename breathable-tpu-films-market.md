<p><p>The breathable TPU films market is segmented into three primary types based on breathability levels: low, medium, and high. Low breathable TPU films offer minimal airflow, suitable for applications requiring moisture resistance and limited breathability. Medium breathable films provide a balanced solution, allowing moderate air exchange while still retaining some moisture barrier properties. High breathable TPU films, designed for optimal airflow, are ideal for applications needing maximum ventilation, such as medical textiles and performance sportswear, enhancing comfort and reducing heat buildup.</p></p>
