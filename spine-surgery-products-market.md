<p><p>The spine surgery products market is categorized into spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are designed to stabilize the spine by fusing vertebrae together, typically using implants such as rods, screws, and bone grafts to promote healing. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, focus on providing stability and pain relief without permanently fusing the spine, often involving devices like artificial discs and instrumentation for motion preservation. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
