<p><ul><li>Blocking Oil Type</li><li>Tube Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper terminals market comprises two main types: Blocking Oil Type and Tube Pressure Type. Blocking Oil Type terminals use oil to create a mechanical barrier, ensuring reliable connections and enhancing electrical performance in various applications. Conversely, Tube Pressure Type terminals utilize a crimping method that applies pressure within a tube to secure the wire connection, offering robustness and resistance to environmental factors. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring efficient and effective electrical connectivity solutions.</p></p>
