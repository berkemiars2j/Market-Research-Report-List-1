<p><p>The homecare oxygen concentrators market comprises two primary types: portable oxygen concentrators and stationary oxygen concentrators. Stationary oxygen concentrators, on the other hand, are designed for home use, providing a continuous supply of oxygen through an electric connection, typically offering higher flow rates and greater output, making them suitable for patients requiring higher oxygen volumes for extended periods.</p></p>
