<p><p>The monitoring camera market is primarily divided into two types: digital monitoring cameras and analog monitoring cameras. Digital monitoring cameras utilize advanced technology, providing higher resolution images, remote access, and enhanced features like motion detection and cloud storage. They are ideal for modern surveillance systems. Conversely, analog monitoring cameras are traditional, using a simpler technology that transmits video signals directly to a recorder. While generally lower in cost, they offer limited features and image quality compared to their digital counterparts.</p></p>
