<p><p>The Extended Reality Display market encompasses various technologies, primarily Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD) and Organic Light-Emitting Diodes (OLED). LCDs utilize a backlight to illuminate pixels, offering bright images and cost-effectiveness, making them popular for mainstream applications. In contrast, OLEDs feature self-emissive pixels, allowing for deeper blacks, higher contrast, and improved color accuracy, making them ideal for high-end applications. Each technology serves distinct needs, influencing consumer choices based on performance, cost, and application suitability in immersive experiences.</p></p>
