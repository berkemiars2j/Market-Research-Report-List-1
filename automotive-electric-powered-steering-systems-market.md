<p><ul><li>Column Type</li><li>Pinion-and-rack Type</li><li>Double pinion Type</li><li>Ball screw Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric power steering (EPS) systems market includes several types, each with unique designs and applications. The column type utilizes an electric motor integrated into the steering column, offering compactness. The pinion-and-rack type features an electric motor attached to the pinion gear, enhancing steering precision. The double pinion type includes two pinion gears for improved responsiveness and feel. The ball screw type uses a ball screw mechanism for efficient torque transfer, optimizing steering ease and driver control.</p></p>
