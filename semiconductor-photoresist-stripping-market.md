<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Stripping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripping</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist stripping market is categorized into two main types: positive photoresist stripping and negative photoresist stripping. Positive photoresist stripping involves removing photoresist layers that become soluble upon exposure to light, allowing for precise patterning in semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, negative photoresist stripping deals with materials that harden when exposed to light, making the unexposed areas removable. Both types are crucial for achieving high-resolution patterns on silicon wafers, facilitating advanced electronic device production.</p></p>
