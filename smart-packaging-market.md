<p><strong>The Smart Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Packaging</li><li>Intelligent Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart packaging encompasses two main types: active packaging and intelligent packaging. Active packaging interacts with the contents or the environment to extend shelf life or enhance food safety. It includes materials that absorb oxygen or emit preservatives. Intelligent packaging, on the other hand, provides real-time information about the condition of the product or its surroundings, often using sensors to monitor freshness, temperature, or humidity. Together, they improve food management, quality assurance, and consumer convenience in the supply chain.</p></p>
