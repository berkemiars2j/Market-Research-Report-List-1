<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiotherapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiotherapy equipment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** involves machines like linear accelerators that deliver high-energy beams to target tumors from outside the body. </p><p>2. **Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** includes technologies such as brachytherapy, where radioactive sources are placed inside or near the tumor.</p><p>3. **Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment** administers radioactive substances through the bloodstream, targeting cancer cells throughout the body. Each type plays a crucial role in cancer treatment, offering tailored options for patient care.</p></p>
