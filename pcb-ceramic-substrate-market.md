<p><p>The PCB ceramic substrate market primarily comprises three types: Al2O3 (alumina), AlN (aluminum nitride), and other advanced materials. Al2O3 is favored for its electrical insulation and thermal stability, making it suitable for various electronics. AlN offers superior thermal conductivity and is ideal for high-performance applications, such as power devices. Other materials may include silicon nitride and various composites, catering to niche applications with specific requirements. This segmentation allows manufacturers to choose substrates based on performance, cost, and application needs.</p></p>
