<p><ul><li>Heat Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterilization technologies encompass various methods to eliminate microorganisms. Heat sterilization utilizes high temperatures, often through steam or dry heat, to achieve effective sterilization, suitable for heat-resistant materials. Low-temperature sterilization employs methods like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide gas plasma, ideal for heat-sensitive items. Other sterilization technologies include chemical, radiation, and filtration methods, providing versatile solutions for different materials and applications. Each method caters to specific requirements, balancing effectiveness, material compatibility, and operational efficiency in diverse settings.</p></p>
