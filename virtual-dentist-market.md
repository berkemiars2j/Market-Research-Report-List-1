<p><p>The virtual dentist market comprises two primary types of consultation services: synchronous and asynchronous. Synchronous consultation involves real-time interactions between dentists and patients, typically through video calls, enabling immediate diagnosis and treatment recommendations. Conversely, asynchronous consultation allows patients to send inquiries or images to dentists, who respond at their convenience. This method provides flexibility for both parties, facilitating initial assessments or follow-ups without the need for simultaneous engagement, thereby catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in dental care.</p></p>
