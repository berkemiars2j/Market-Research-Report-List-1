<p><p>The cancer molecular biomarkers market can be categorized into three main types: genetic methods, protein methods, and imaging techniques. Genetic methods focus on identifying specific DNA or RNA alterations associated with cancer, aiding in early detection and treatment personalization. Protein methods analyze biomarkers like enzymes or hormones to assess tumor presence and behavior. Imaging methods utilize advanced technologies, such as PET and MRI, to visualize tumors and monitor treatment responses, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and therapeutic management.</p></p>
