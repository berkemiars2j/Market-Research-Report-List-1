<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Mist Cooling System</li><li>Medium Pressure Mist Cooling System</li><li>High Pressure Mist Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial mist cooling system market consists of three main types based on operating pressure: low, medium, and high pressure. Low pressure mist cooling systems typically operate below 50 psi and are suited for smaller areas, offering cost-effective solutions. Medium pressure systems range from 50 to 150 psi, providing enhanced cooling for medium-sized spaces. High pressure systems operate above 150 psi, delivering finer mist particles for efficient cooling in large areas, often used in industrial and outdoor settings to combat heat effectively.</p></p>
