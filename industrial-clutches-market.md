<p><p>The industrial clutches market comprises three main types: electromagnetic, hydraulic, and pneumatic clutches. Electromagnetic clutches use electrical signals to engage or disengage, offering precise control in various applications. Hydraulic clutches utilize fluid pressure, providing high torque and smooth operation, ideal for heavy machinery. Pneumatic clutches employ compressed air for engagement, known for their quick response and lightweight design, often used in automation and conveyor systems. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing operational efficiency and performance.</p></p>
