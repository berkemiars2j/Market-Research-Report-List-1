<p><p>The UV cured resin market comprises several key components: oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers serve as the backbone for UV curing, providing flexibility and strength. Monomers enhance the physical properties and facilitate the curing process. Photoinitiators are crucial for triggering the polymerization process when exposed to UV light. Additives include colorants, stabilizers, and fillers to modify the final product's performance and appearance. Together, these elements contribute to diverse applications in industries like coatings, adhesives, and 3D printing.</p></p>
