<p><p>The Wet-End Paper Making Starches market includes Wet End Starch and Wet End Cationic Starch, both vital in the paper production process. Wet End Starch enhances sheet strength, moisture retention, and printability, improving overall paper quality. In contrast, Wet End Cationic Starch, positively charged, enhances bonding properties between fibers and fillers, resulting in better retention of materials and increased consistency.
