<p><p>The airport navaids market comprises various systems essential for navigation and landing. Instrument Landing Systems (ILS) provide precision guidance during approach and landing. Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) offers accurate distance information to pilots. Non-Directional Beacons (NDB) send out signals for navigation assistance, while VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) allows for determining aircraft position. The Ground-Based Augmentation System (GBAS) enhances accuracy for landings. Other navaids may include satellite-based systems and visual aids, supporting safe and efficient air traffic operations.</p></p>
