<p><ul><li>Gauze Plaster Cast</li><li>Elastic Plaster Cast</li><li>Polymer Plaster Cast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plaster cast market includes three main types: gauze plaster casts, elastic plaster casts, and polymer plaster casts. Gauze plaster casts are traditional, made from plaster-soaked gauze, providing support but limited flexibility. Elastic plaster casts incorporate elastic materials, allowing for easier mobility and safer application, making them suitable for various injuries. Polymer plaster casts utilize advanced materials like fiberglass, offering lightweight, durable, and water-resistant options, catering to patients who require long-term immobilization with enhanced comfort and convenience.</p></p>
