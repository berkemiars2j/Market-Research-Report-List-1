<p><p>The tele-operated marine drone market is primarily categorized into two types: monohull and catamaran designs. Monohull drones feature a single hull, offering agility and ease of maneuverability, making them suitable for various marine operations. In contrast, catamaran drones consist of two parallel hulls, providing greater stability and load capacity, which is advantageous for tasks requiring heavier equipment or longer operational durations. Both designs cater to different operational needs and environments within the marine industry.</p></p>
