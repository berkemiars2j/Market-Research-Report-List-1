<p><p>The Erythrocyte Sedimentation Tube (EST) market can be categorized into two main types: plastic and glass. Plastic tubes are preferred for their lightweight, durability, and reduced risk of breakage, making them ideal for high-volume laboratories. They also offer convenience in disposal. On the other hand, glass tubes, though heavier and more fragile, provide superior clarity and stability for observance of sedimentation rates. Both types serve critical roles in diagnosing inflammatory and autoimmune conditions through sedimentation rate testing.</p></p>
