<p><strong>The Shaking Table Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary</li><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Double Function</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shaking table markets can be categorized into three main types: Rotary, Reciprocating, and Double Function. </p><p>Rotary shaking tables utilize a spinning mechanism to enhance material separation based on density. Reciprocating shaking tables use a back-and-forth motion, promoting effective stratification and allowing for better recovery of valuable materials. Double Function tables combine the features of both rotary and reciprocating designs, maximizing efficiency and versatility for various mining and material processing applications. Each type serves unique operational needs in mineral separation.</p></p>
