<p><p>The nickel alloy welding consumables market is primarily categorized into Monel and Inconel alloys. Monel alloys, primarily composed of nickel and copper, are known for their excellent resistance to corrosion and high temperatures, making them ideal for marine and chemical processing applications. Inconel alloys, rich in nickel and chromium, exhibit exceptional strength and oxidation resistance at elevated temperatures. These traits make Inconel suitable for aerospace, nuclear, and various industrial applications, driving demand in specialized welding projects across industries.</p></p>
