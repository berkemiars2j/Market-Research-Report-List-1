<p><p>Walk Through Metal Detectors (WTMD) are classified into two main types based on their detection zones: Single Zone and Multi Zone. Single Zone detectors offer a basic level of security by detecting metal objects as individuals pass through a single scanning area. In contrast, Multi Zone detectors provide enhanced security with multiple detection zones, allowing for more precise identification of the location and size of metal objects on a person. This makes Multi Zone detectors more effective in high-security environments.</p></p>
