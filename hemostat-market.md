<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Flowable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemostat market is categorized into two main types: mechanical and flowable. Mechanical hemostats involve physical devices like clips, sponges, or gauze that exert pressure to control bleeding. These are typically easy to use and provide immediate results. Flowable hemostats, on the other hand, consist of liquid formulations that can be applied directly to the wound site, promoting clot formation through biochemical means. Both types are essential in surgical procedures for effective blood management and ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
