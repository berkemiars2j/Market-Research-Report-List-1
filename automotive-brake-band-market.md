<p><p>The automotive brake band market is primarily segmented into two types: simple brake bands and differential brake bands. Simple brake bands are straightforward components that engage the brake drum to provide stopping power, often used in a variety of vehicles for basic braking needs. In contrast, differential brake bands are designed for more complex applications, allowing for precise control over braking in systems like automatic transmissions, where differential torque is required for effective operation. Both types play critical roles in vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
