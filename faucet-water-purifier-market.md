<p><p>The Faucet Water Purifier market encompasses three primary types: Faucet Mounted, Counter Top, and Built-In systems. Faucet Mounted purifiers attach directly to the existing tap, offering convenient filtration for drinking water. Counter Top models are standalone units placed beside the sink, providing more extensive filtration capabilities and larger capacities. Built-In purifiers are integrated into the plumbing, offering a seamless design and continuous access to purified water without the need for separate installations or additional counter space.</p></p>
