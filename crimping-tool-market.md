<p><ul><li>Manual Crimping Tool</li><li>Hydraulic Crimping Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crimping tool market is divided into two main types: manual crimping tools and hydraulic crimping tools. Manual crimping tools operate through hand pressure, making them suitable for smaller tasks and offering portability. They are often used for light applications and in environments where ease of use is crucial. In contrast, hydraulic crimping tools utilize hydraulic power for greater force, allowing them to handle larger cables and connectors. These tools are ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications requiring consistent and reliable crimping.</p></p>
