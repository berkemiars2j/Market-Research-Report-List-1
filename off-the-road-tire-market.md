<p><p>The Off-the-Road (OTR) tire market features three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing better traction and shock absorption, ideal for uneven terrains. Solid tires, made from a single piece of rubber, are puncture-resistant and durable, commonly used in harsh environments. Polyurethane tires combine flexibility and resilience, offering reduced weight and superior load handling. Each type is tailored for specific applications, balancing performance, durability, and safety in off-road conditions.</p></p>
