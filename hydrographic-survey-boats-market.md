<p><p>The hydrographic survey boats market can be categorized into two main types: monohull and multihull boats. Monohull hydrographic survey boats feature a single hull design, offering stability and ease of maneuverability, ideal for shallow waters. In contrast, multihull hydrographic survey boats, which consist of two or more hulls, provide enhanced stability, reduced drag, and larger deck space, making them suitable for offshore and rougher water conditions. Both types are essential for conducting detailed underwater surveys and data collection.</p></p>
