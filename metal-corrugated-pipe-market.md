<p><p>The metal corrugated pipe market primarily consists of two types: single wall corrugated and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes feature a simple, flexible design for efficient drainage and lightweight applications. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes possess an inner and outer layer, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-load environments. Both types are widely used in construction, infrastructure, and agricultural sectors for water management, structural support, and erosion control.</p></p>
